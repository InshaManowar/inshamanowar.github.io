Projects

What matters is how one applies their skills and knowledge

Sociio Ichor

Sociio Ichor is a blood donation portal that aims to bridge the gap between donors and patients. In collaboration with KMC, Manipal, the Sociio Ichor team works towards incorporating blood donation as a part of lifestyle. The website is built in Django, HTML, and CSS. Its made responsive using Bootstrap. The website follows good SEO practices as well.

Ayurved Proctology Association

Ayurved Procotology Asociation is a mumbai based Ayurved Hospital. The website aims to provide the association with an online presence and help gain members. It also aims to promote the aim of the hospital. The website is built in Django along with HTML, CSS and Bootstrap. Members can login and view member-exclusive content easily.

Summer School - ISTE Manipal

Summer School is an annual event of ISTE Manipal, one of the largest technical society at MIT, Manipal. It saw over 450 participations over a span of 3 months. Students have to login to the portal, view tasks uploaded by organisers, complete the task and submit it on the website. The organisers can correct the course and send comments to the student. A certificate is auto generated on completion of all tasks. The website is made in Django REST framework.

Grade Calculator

When college devised a formula to prorate grades of students, a friend an I decided to develop a simple calculator for it using Django. The portal gained a lot of popularity and had 10k+ visits in less than 6 hours
